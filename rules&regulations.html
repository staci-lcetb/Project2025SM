Rules & Regulations

Rules

  1. Punctuality: Please ensure that you arrive on time for training.
  2. Space: Leave enough space between other members and machine/equipment.
  3. Phones: Keep phones in the assigned lockers.
  4. Safety: Lock-in all equipment and machines before and after use.
  5. Participation: Respect machines and equipment.
  6. Hygiene: Clean up your work space after use.
  7. Duty: Put all equipment back after use and re-rack weights.
  8. Remember: Return Locker Keys and leave no rubbish behind.

Regulations

Phat Gym has obtained all necessary certifications and licensing. We have liable insurance of the property and we offer our members a small fee for insurance while training at our gym. This fee is not mandatory but for those who wish to be insured can sign up.

Our trainers are trained and certified in CPR & AED. All members will be giving an induction on the proper use of the gym equipment and machines. Towel dry the equipment/machines after use. Membership entry begins at 16 years any persons 18 or under will need a parent/guardian signature.

The building has CCTV inside and outside to ensure safety and security. Health Screening forms will be asked and required from all members. Lockers are available for the period of class or work out session, all belongings must be removed when class or session is over and the locker key returned to reception.

We have a Zero Tolerance for bad language or aggressive behaviour towards staff or other members. This behaviour can lead to termination of membership and a ban from the premises. Any concerns can be reported to a member of staff.

Smoking and Vaping are not permitted in the building members can do so outside away from the front of the gym. Cigarette users are asked to bin their butts.

A consent form will be giving to members to have our staff record their progress at Phat Gym. Body Building members can enquire from their assigned trainer details of competition entries if desired.

We ask that all members and staff co-operate with these guides set out for Phat Gym.
